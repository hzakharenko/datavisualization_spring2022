The bigger the penguin, the bigger the flipper length

The different types of Penguin species examined in this chart are: Adelie Torgersen, Adelie Biscoe, Chinstrap Dream, Adelie Dream and Gentoo Biscoe. The larger the species is (body mass) the longer their flipper length is.
Source: https://github.com/ttimbers/palmerpenguins/

Different penguin breeds have equal male/female populations

The different types of Penguin species examined in this chart are: Adelie Torgersen, Adelie Biscoe, Chinstrap Dream, Adelie Dream and Gentoo Biscoe. In all the Adelie species that this sample collected, there was an equal amount of male and female penguins.
